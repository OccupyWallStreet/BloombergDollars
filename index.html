How Much is a Bloomberg Buck?

200,000X Wealthier Than the Average US Family

For every dollar in your wallet, Mike Bloomberg has $197,916. In other words, something that would be worth $1 to Mike Bloomberg would be worth just under $200,000 to you.

200,000 times wealthier than
the average American Family

With a net worth around 19 billion dollars, Bloomberg is America's wealthiest politician and one of the wealthiest people in America, ranked as high as #8 in recent years. The median net worth (income, property, investments) of an American family on the other hand is about $96,000. And remember, that's the median; half of Americans have less money that that.
